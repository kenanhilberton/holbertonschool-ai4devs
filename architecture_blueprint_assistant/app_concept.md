# Application Concept – EcoRide Connect

## Application
EcoRide Connect is a smart carpooling platform designed to optimize urban commuting by matching drivers with passengers in real-time, focusing on reducing carbon footprints and travel costs.

## Core Features
- **Smart Route Matching**: AI algorithms to find the most efficient pickup and drop-off points.
- **Eco-Impact Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of CO2 emissions saved by each carpool session.
- **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: Automated cost-sharing calculations based on distance, fuel prices, and tolls.
- **In-App Secure Payments**: Integrated digital wallet and automated split-payment system.

## Users
- **Drivers**: Individuals looking to share fuel costs and reduce the boredom of solo commuting.
- **Passengers**: Commuters looking for an affordable, eco-friendly alternative to public transport or taxis.
- **Corporate Admins**: Company representatives managing employee carpooling programs to meet sustainability goals.

## Constraints
- **Scalability**: The system must handle high traffic during peak hours (up to 200K concurrent users).
- **Compliance**: Adherence to local transport regulations and strict GDPR data protection for user location history.
- **Accessibility**: A mobile-first, high-performance interface optimized for low-latency updates on the move.
