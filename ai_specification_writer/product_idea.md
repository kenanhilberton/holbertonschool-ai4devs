# Product Idea: TaskFlow AI

## Vision
To streamline team productivity by using AI to automatically categorize, prioritize, and assign tasks based on team members' expertise and current workload.

## Users
- **Project Managers**: Need a high-level view of progress and automated task distribution to avoid bottlenecks.
- **Developers/Team Members**: Need a clear, prioritized list of tasks that match their skills without manual searching.
- **Stakeholders**: Need automated reports on project timelines and completion estimates.

## Key Features
- **AI Task Prioritization**: Automatically ranks tasks based on urgency and impact.
- **Smart Assignment**: Matches tasks to the best-suited team member using historical data.
- **Workload Balancing**: Prevents burnout by monitoring and adjusting individual task loads in real-time.
- **Automated Progress Reports**: Generates weekly summaries of achievements and upcoming risks.
