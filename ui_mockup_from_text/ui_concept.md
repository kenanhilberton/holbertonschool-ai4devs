# UI Concept - EcoRide Connect (Carpooling App)

## Purpose
To provide a seamless, eco-friendly ride-sharing experience by connecting drivers with passengers traveling on the same route, reducing carbon emissions and travel costs.

## Users
- **Commuters:** Daily office-goers or travelers looking for affordable and social shared transport options.[cite: 1]
- **Drivers:** Car owners who want to share empty seats to offset fuel costs and reduce environmental impact.[cite: 1]

## Core Screens
- **Landing Dashboard:** Displays nearby available rides, quick-search bar, and current sustainability stats (CO2 saved).[cite: 1]
- **Ride Search & Booking:** Map-based interface to select routes, view driver ratings, and book seats.[cite: 1]
- **Ride Posting (Driver Mode):** A form for drivers to set routes, time, price per seat, and vehicle details.[cite: 1]
- **User Profile & Wallet:** Personal details, ride history, and digital wallet for payments and earnings.[cite: 1]

## Actions
- **Search for Rides:** Enter destination and time to find matches.[cite: 1]
- **Book a Seat:** Secure a spot in a car and initiate payment.[cite: 1]
- **Post a Ride:** Drivers publish their itinerary for others to join.[cite: 1]
- **Track Impact:** View personal and community CO2 reduction metrics.[cite: 1]
