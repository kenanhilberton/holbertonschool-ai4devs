# UI Concept - EcoRide Connect (Carpooling App)

## Purpose
To provide a seamless, eco-friendly ride-sharing experience by connecting drivers with passengers traveling on the same route, reducing carbon emissions and travel costs.

## Users
- **Commuters:** Daily office-goers looking for affordable and social travel options.
- **Eco-conscious travelers:** Individuals prioritizing shared transport to reduce their carbon footprint.
- **Drivers:** Car owners looking to offset fuel costs by sharing empty seats.

## Core Screens
- **Landing Dashboard:** Displays nearby available rides, quick-search bar, and current sustainability stats (CO2 saved).
- **Ride Search & Booking:** Map-based interface to select routes, view driver ratings, and book seats.
- **Ride Posting (Driver Mode):** A form for drivers to set routes, time, price per seat, and vehicle details.
- **User Profile & Wallet:** Personal details, ride history, and digital wallet for payments and earnings.

## Actions
- **Search for Rides:** Enter destination and time to find matches.
- **Book a Seat:** Secure a spot in a car and initiate payment.
- **Post a Ride:** Drivers publish their itinerary for others to join.
- **Track Impact:** View personal and community CO2 reduction metrics.
