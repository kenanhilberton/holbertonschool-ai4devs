# UI Concept - EcoRide Connect

## Purpose
To provide a seamless, eco-friendly ride-sharing experience by connecting drivers with passengers traveling on the same route, reducing carbon emissions and travel costs.

## Users
- **Commuters:** Individuals looking for affordable and social shared transport options.
- **Drivers:** Car owners who want to share empty seats to offset fuel costs.

## Core Screens
- **Landing Dashboard:** Displays nearby available rides and sustainability stats.
- **Ride Search & Booking:** Map-based interface to find and book seats.
- **Ride Posting:** A form for drivers to set routes, time, and seat availability.

## Actions
- **Search for Rides:** Enter destination and time to find matches.
- **Book a Seat:** Secure a spot in a car and initiate payment.
- **Post a Ride:** Drivers publish their itinerary for others to join.
